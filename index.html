A Day in the Life of the Internet

Midnight

Much of the world sleeps, but streaming platforms and gaming servers remain active late into the night.

Morning

As Europe wakes, productivity tools, email servers, and work platforms begin to dominate internet activity.

Afternoon

The internet becomes a workplace. Collaboration tools and cloud services carry the rhythm of the global workday.

Evening

Entertainment returns. Streaming, social media, and gaming surge as people unwind after work.

Late Night

Activity quiets again, but the network never sleeps.
